About Me

Education: I gathered my master's degree in astronomy and astrophysics in 2022 and have one year of experience doing a PhD in astrophysics at Leiden University. I specialize in data analysis, 2D and 3D modelling, simulations and general physics. My nische experience in astrophysics revolves around predicting stellar dynamical motions (Bachelor's degree), prediciting existence of black holes and intermediate mass black holes in globular clusters, specifically under the influence of hierarchial systems (Master's degree) to 3D magnetohydrodynamic modelling of coronal mass ejections (PhD). In the recent year, I have worked as a system test engineer at SAAB AB, which comprises both hardware and software management and development of defence system components.
